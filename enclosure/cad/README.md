# Enclosure — Chameleon Mesh Node v1

Planned post-fab. The enclosure design depends on the final routed PCB and antenna pigtail length, neither of which is locked yet.

Target form factor:

- 3D-printable in ASA or polycarbonate for outdoor UV/weather tolerance
- Vented for the BME280 sensor (one face has a small slot with a Gore-Tex-equivalent membrane)
- Two antenna passthroughs (sealed RP-SMA bulkheads): one for the LoRa whip, one optional for an external 2.4 GHz antenna if the operator chooses a directional Wi-Fi/BLE configuration
- Solar panel mount on the lid: 60 × 40 mm flush-mounted cell pocket with a 4-screw retention pattern
- IP65 minimum gasket-sealed seam
- Battery pocket sized for a single 18650 cell holder OR a flat LiPo pouch up to 60 × 30 × 6 mm

Files (STEP + STL + FreeCAD source) will land here before final fab submission.
